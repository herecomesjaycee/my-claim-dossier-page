My Claim Dossier

Disrupting the healthcare insurance market

The journey

The idea was born at an weekend of working together at Break The Block, a blockchain in insurance hackathon. We had fun hacking and we created My Claim Dossier which won ‘Best Blockchain’ and ‘Best Concept’ at Break The Block.

What does it do?

My Claim Dossier is built on a concept of distributed ledger architecture that can retain any customer’s full claims history. This enables insurers to create fairer alternative pricing strategies and allow customers to shop for better quotes without incurring the administrative costs that rigorous screening measures entail. We believe that with My Claim Dossier, that smaller insurers could club together to cut costs of storing information on a large customer database.
